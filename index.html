Designed for practical publishing

A focused viewer for making PDF experiences feel modern.

Inspired by interactive publishing platforms, Flip-a-Thing keeps the workflow lightweight: bring your PDF, embed the viewer, and give readers a better way to browse.

Page-flip presentation

Give static PDFs a publication-style reading experience with animated page turning and visual navigation.

Reader controls

Zoom, fullscreen, previous and next controls, and mouse or pinch gestures are built into each viewer instance.

Accessible escape hatch

Each flipbook keeps a direct accessible PDF view available for assistive technology and standard document workflows.

Multiple viewers

Drop multiple instances onto the same page and point each one at a different PDF without ID collisions.
